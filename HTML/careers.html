Culinary Kitchen Staff

Interested in enhancing your culinary skills! Want to get your culinary career moving, by assisting with all aspect of food preparation? Can you support and assist an Executive Chef? Then perhaps Eloquent Creations Catering Service is the place for you. If you thrive in a high quality and fast-paced,environment, you’ll thrive in the ECCS Kitchen. Familiarity with various kitchen stations and cooking techniques are required. Availability for early mornings and late evenings, Monday through Saturday or as scheduled. Please forward email to: info@eloquentcreationscatering.com

Event Staff

Eloquent Creations & Design is looking for professional captains, waiters, waitresses and licensed bartenders to offer competent and friendly service to our growing clientele. All positions require great attitudes and excellent presentation. We are also looking for experienced Chefs with a professional demeanor and polished presentation. If you enjoy teamwork and interacting with people, this may be the job for you. Please forward email to: info@eloquentcreationscatering.com

Internships

Are you seeking to gain work experience to help secure a job after graduation? The kitchen is growing at ECCS and we are looking for passionate, energetic and time conscious interns to join our team. Interns would be responsible for assisting the chef with prepping, processing and preparing food for delivery. Please forward email to: info@eloquentcreationscatering.com
