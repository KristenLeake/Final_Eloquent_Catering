Careers

SALES

Are you a self-starter who appreciates the benefits of a team environment? Are you detail-oriented, outgoing, and able to enthusiastically convey the benefits of Windows Catering Company? If so, we want to talk to you about joining our sales team. Previous catering/hospitality experience is a plus.
Please forward email to velliott@eloquentcreationscatering.com

KITCHEN STAFF

Interested in enhancing your culinary skills as a Sous Chef, while working with the area’s premier chefs? Want to get your culinary career moving as a Line Cook, by assisting with all aspect of food preparation? Then perhaps Windows Catering Company is the place for you. Owner/Chef Henry Dinardo, one of Food & Wine’s Top Chefs in America, oversees our state of the art kitchen, now the largest in the area. If you thrive in a fast-paced, high quality environment, you’ll thrive in the Windows kitchen. Please forward email to velliott@eloquentcreationscatering.com

EVENT STAFF

Windows Catering Company is looking for professional captains, waiters, waitresses and bartenders to offer competent and friendly service to our growing clientele. All positions require a tuxedo and excellent presentation. We are also looking for experienced Off-Premise Chefs with a professional demeanor and polished presentation. If you enjoy teamwork and interacting with people, this may be the job for you.
Please forward email to velliott@eloquentcreationscatering.com
